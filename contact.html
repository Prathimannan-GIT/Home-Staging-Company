Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

How long does the staging process take?

Physical staging typically takes 1-3 days depending on the property size. Virtual staging can be completed within 48 hours.

What is included in the staging service?

Our services include furniture rental, accessories, artwork, lighting design, and professional installation. We also provide photography coordination.

Do you offer virtual staging?

Yes! We offer high-quality virtual staging with photorealistic results. Perfect for vacant properties or when you need quick results.

What areas do you serve?

We primarily serve the Greater Los Angeles area, including Beverly Hills, Santa Monica, Malibu, and surrounding communities.
